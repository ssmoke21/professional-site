About

I am an Informatics Pharmacist at RWJBarnabas Health in West Orange, New Jersey, where I focus on leveraging technology and artificial intelligence to advance pharmacy practice and patient care.

With over a decade of experience as an antimicrobial stewardship pharmacist, I have built deep expertise in infectious diseases, clinical decision support, and medication analytics. My transition into informatics reflects a commitment to using data-driven and AI-powered tools to transform healthcare delivery.

I hold a Doctor of Pharmacy from Rutgers University, Ernest Mario School of Pharmacy, a Master of Science in Health Informatics & Analytics from the University of Arizona, and completed my PGY-1 Pharmacy Practice Residency at the University Medical Center of Princeton at Plainsboro.
